#+startup: showall
#+TAGS: oneshot(o) hand(h) promote(p)

Repo-level backlog, cumulative across initiatives. Items land here from PLAN Deferred sections at initiative close, from ad-hoc discovery (org-capture or mid-session), and from work surfaced out of scope during execution. Items leave by being done, claimed by an initiative, or deliberately dropped.

Size tags: oneshot (compiled single-session prompt), hand (inert edit, no session), promote (candidate for initiative-scale work). Untagged means untriaged — sizing happens at review or pull time, not capture time.
